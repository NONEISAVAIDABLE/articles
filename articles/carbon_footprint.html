5 Quick Steps to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

By Brycen Weeks

  1. Use a reusable water bottle
  2. Ride a bike around town
  3. Turn off lights when your not using them
  4. Keep the tires on your car in good shape!
  5. Eat more locally grown food
